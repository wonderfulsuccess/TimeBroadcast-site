Privacy Policy

Simple data handling for a local reminder app.

Time Broadcast Assistant is designed to work primarily on device. The current version does not collect personal data from inside the app for analytics, advertising, or profiling.

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. What the app does

Time Broadcast Assistant lets you define a start time, end time, repeat interval, and custom spoken reminder. At each scheduled checkpoint, the app can speak the current time together with your message.

2. Data the app stores on device

  • Schedule settings such as start time, end time, repeat interval, repeat mode, enable state, and announcement text.
  • Temporary generated audio used for scheduled reminder playback.
  • Notification permission state and related local scheduling information.

These items stay on your device unless you manually share information through a support channel.

3. Data the app does not collect

  • No account information is required.
  • No analytics SDK, advertising SDK, crash reporting SDK, or third-party tracker is included.
  • No user content is uploaded to a remote server by the app.
  • No data is sold or used for targeted advertising.

4. Notifications

Notification permission is requested only when lock-screen reminder behavior is needed. Notifications are used to deliver local reminder alerts and expose the stop action while a schedule is running.

5. Support communications

If you contact support through the public channels listed on the Support page, you may choose to share information such as your email address or issue details. That information is used only to respond to your support request and is not collected in-app automatically.

6. Third-party services

The current app does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, social login, or cloud sync services. If you open external links such as GitHub from the Support page, those services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Data retention and deletion

Local schedule data remains on the device until you change it, turn the schedule off, or remove the app. Deleting the app removes its locally stored settings and temporary audio files.

8. Children and family use

The app may be used in family routine scenarios, but it is not a medical, educational assessment, or behavioral treatment tool.

9. Changes to this policy

If the app’s data practices change, this page will be updated before or together with the relevant app release.

10. Contact

For privacy questions, use the public support contact information listed on the Support page. Replace the placeholder support details there before submitting the app for review.
