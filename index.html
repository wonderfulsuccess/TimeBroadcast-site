How It Works

Designed for repeatable, spoken checkpoints.

  • Announcements in the foreground use the system voice directly.
  • When background announcements are enabled, the app keeps an active audio session so scheduled speech can continue after you minimize the app or lock the device.
  • If you force-quit the app from the app switcher, background spoken announcements stop and only the local notification remains.
  • Background announcements can increase battery usage.

Privacy By Default

No account, no ads, no third-party tracking.

  • Schedule settings and temporary generated audio are stored only on your device.
  • The app does not require a login, cloud sync, subscription, or remote service.
  • Notifications are requested only when lock-screen reminder behavior is needed.
  • The current version does not collect data for analytics, advertising, or profiling.
